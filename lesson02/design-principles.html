Visual Hierarchy

Bob's Power Center

https://bobspowercenter.com/

This complies with the principle of visual hierarchy because the elements are arranged in such a way that they are easy to observe, pleasing to the eye, and simple to identify. They have a good size and position, with effective use of color and contrast, visually pleasing typography, and proper spacing. Additionally, there are icons and images that aid in identification.

Hick's Law

Buffalo River Sales

https://www.buffaloriversales.com/

Here, Hick's Law is fulfilled as there is a good grouping and categorization of elements, each appropriately prioritized. There are dropdown menus and filters, along with a minimalist design. In conclusion, the site has what is necessary without making it cluttered to the eye and easy to appreciate.

Fitt's Law

Christian Brothers Equipment of Kentucky

https://www.christianbrosequip.com/

Here, Fitts's Law is applied, as the elements within the website have appropriate margins, padding, and overall spacing to comply with this law. This will enable the user to reduce the time in decision-making while navigating the site. Therefore, the arrangement of interface elements is optimized, including button placement and overall interactivity of the site.

Final Project - Mower Medics Site Plan

Site Name: Mower Medics

The name have been chosen to convey the idea that the company offers "medicine" or specialized care for lawn mowers and gardening equipment, emphasizing precision and quality in the maintenance and repair of these tools. The term "Medics" can also evoke the idea of expert and prompt care, similar to how doctors care for people's health.

Site Purpose:

Provide Information: The website aims to provide customers with detailed information about the company's products and services, including new equipment, inventory, and available services. This helps potential customers make informed decisions about their outdoor power equipment needs.

Promote Engagement: By featuring in-stock inventory, special offers, and financing options, the site aims to engage customers and encourage them to explore the range of products and services offered by Mower Medics. This can lead to increased sales and customer loyalty.

Color Schema:

Primary Color: (#0150EB): Used for headings and accents to symbolize the bright and positive community spirit.

Secondary Color: (#CD2027): Used for background colors and subtle accents, representing the clear skies over Sunshine Valley.

Prime Color Contrast: #fff a white as always is needed.

Second Color Contrast: #1E1E1E a black as always is needed.

Typography:

Heading Font: Oswald: Used for all headings to provide a modern and clean look.

Body Font: Open Sans: Ensures readability and compatibility across various devices.

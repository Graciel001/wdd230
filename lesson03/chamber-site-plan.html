L03: Project Milestone - The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name: Sunshine Valley Chamber of Commerce

Named after the picturesque town of Sunshine Valley, known for its vibrant community, beautiful landscapes, and thriving local businesses.

Site Purpose:

a. To promote local businesses and foster economic growth in Sunshine Valley.

b. To provide information on community events, attractions, and resources.

Scenarios:

a. "What local events are happening this month that could benefit my business through networking opportunities?"

b. "Where can I find contact information for the members of the Sunshine Valley Chamber of Commerce?"

c. "Can you provide information on the recent population growth in Sunshine Valley?"

Color Schema:

Primary Color: Sunshine Yellow (#f1b85b): Used for headings and accents to symbolize the bright and positive community spirit.

Secondary Color: (#322e29): Used for background colors and subtle accents, representing the clear skies over Sunshine Valley.

Prime Color Contrast: #fff a white as always is needed.

Second Color Contrast Sky Blue: #000 a black as always is needed.

Typography:

Heading Font: Montserrat: Used for all headings to provide a modern and clean look.

Body Font: Open Sans: Ensures readability and compatibility across various devices.
